About Me

Hello! I'm Rajarohan, a passionate developer with a focus on creating innovative solutions across various platforms. I specialize in Android development, web development, AI, and machine learning.

My journey began with a deep interest in technology and a desire to learn how things work. Over the years, I have honed my skills in programming languages such as Java, Python, and JavaScript, and have gained expertise in frameworks like React and TensorFlow.

I am currently pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning from SRU Warangal. I have a CGPA of 9.5 and have actively participated in various projects, hackathons, and internships that have helped me grow both personally and professionally.

My goal is to leverage my skills and knowledge to create impactful solutions that can make a difference in the world. Whether it's building a mobile application, developing a machine learning model, or creating a responsive website, I am always excited to take on new challenges and learn from them.
